Welcome to The Spider-Man Playbook! Here, you'll find a catalogue and description of all known tech and the most viable/interesting combos. The playbook is customisable to suit your preferences, so if this is your first visit, you will likely find the video here on the homepage helpful.

This website draws inspiration from Icegawd's "The Amazing Spiderman Playbook" while aiming to make the information more accessible and digestible to broad audiences.

If you're aware of any tech or combos which are not present here, and would like to see them added, please email them to TheEvilDuck14@gmail.com or direct message them to evilduck_ on Discord.
