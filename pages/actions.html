Punch

Damage: 27.5

Range: 3m

Strengths: Procs tracers for large burst damage

Weaknesses: Very short range, and requires tracers to do any meaningful damage

Notation: punch

Kick

Damage: 44

Range: 4m

Strengths: Increased range over Punch

Weaknesses: Slower than punch, and the increased damgage over Punch hits virtually no new kill thresholds

Notation: kick

Overhead Slam

Damage: 55

Range: 4m

Strengths: Very generous hitbox when up close, increased damage over Punch does hit new kill thresholds

Weaknesses: Slowest startup in Spider-Man's kit, requires setup

Notation: overhead

Uppercut

Damage: 60.5

Range: 4m

Strengths: Can farm ult charge on multiple targets, knocks up, chains very naturally from Get Over Here Targetting

Weaknesses: Reasonably long startup and movement which matches knock-up can enable retaliation

Notation: upper

Tracer

Damage: 33

Range: ∞ (50% falloff from 20-40m)

Strengths: Can be ripped by melee attacks for 49.5 burst damage, useful for animation cancelling, and enables Get Over Here Targetting

Weaknesses: Low individual damage, limited charges

Notation: tracer

Web Swing

Damage: N/A

Range: 30m

Strengths: Incredibly fast movement, grants Overhead Slam, refreshes Double Jump, animation cancels all non-swinging actions

Weaknesses: Slower startup than other heroes' movement abilities, too valuable to be reckless with cooldowns

Notation: swing

Web Zip

Damage: N/A

Range: 30m

Strengths: Grants directional speed very quickly

Weaknesses: Gives less control & adaptability than a high swing

Notation: zip

Web Whiff

Damage: N/A

Range: N/A

Strengths: Delays the granting of Overhead Slam, making unique timings/preserves possible

Weaknesses: Slow recovery into punches, doesn't refresh Double Jump

Notation: whiff

Get Over Here

Damage: 27.5

Range: 20m

Strengths: Stuns and displaces target, closes distance to enable short ranged attacks

Weaknesses: Requires precise aim, cooldown is easily wasted

Notation: goh

Get Over Here Targetting

Damage: 55

Range: 20m

Strengths: Very consistent for closing distance & bursting a marked target, slight knock-up cancels movement abilities

Weaknesses: Easy to read and retaliate

Notation: goht

Jump

Damage: N/A

Range: N/A

Strengths: Enables double jump/movement options and helps evasion

Weaknesses: Reduces micro-manuverability for staying close to a target

Notation: jump

Double Jump

Damage: N/A

Range: N/A

Strengths: Double jumping controls momentum & airtime, helps evasion, and grants an Overhead Slam

Weaknesses: Often moves you out of range for Spider-Man's other actions

Notation: dj
